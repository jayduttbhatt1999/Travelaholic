Niagara Falls

Some Interesting Insight

The Niagara River flows approximately 35 miles/hour (56.3 kilometres/hour). Niagara has two waterfalls, the American Falls and the Canadian Horseshoe Falls. The Falls at Niagara are about 12,000 years old.
