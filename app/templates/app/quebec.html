About Citadelle de Quebec

Some Interesting Insight

This National Historic Site is located atop Cap Diamant in Old Québec. Known as "the Gibraltar of the Americas" for its strategic location, the Citadel has the star-shaped form characteristic of Vauban fortifications. Breathtaking view of the city and the St. Lawrence River.

"Enjoy the view with your Beau"

About Château Frontenac

Some Interesting Insight

The Château Frontenac is one of Canada’s excellent railroad inns. The Château was formally recognised as a National Historic Site of Canada. It is also a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

"Feel an exquisite hint of Europe"
